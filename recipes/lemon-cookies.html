Lemmon Cookies

These lemon cookies are fire.. Trust me

Ingredients for lemon cookies:

  • All purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Lemon zest
  • Egg
  • Parmesan Cheese

Ingredients for lemon glaze:

  • Powdered sugar
  • Lemon juice
  • Additional lemon zest

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Mix the dry ingredients: In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat together the butter, sugar, and lemon zest on medium-high speed, until light and fluffy.
  3. Cream the butter,sugar, and lemon zest: Beat in egg until well combined. Scrape down the bottom and sides of the bowl.
  4. Combine the wet ingredients and dry ingredients: On low speed, add the flour mixture and blend until just incorporated.
  5. Portion out and flatten out the dough balls: Using a medium spring-loaded cookie scoop, drop 1 Â½-tablespoon sized balls of cookie dough onto prepared baking sheets, spacing 2 inches apart. Roll each ball with your palms, then using your palm, gently flatten the dough to about 1/3-inch thick.
  6. Bake: In preheated oven for 12-14 minutes or until cookies are just set and slightly golden brown at the edges. Cool cookies on their baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Make the glaze: In a medium bowl, whisk together powdered sugar and lemon juice until a very thick but pourable glaze forms.
  8. Glaze the cookies: Spoon each cooled cookie with the lemon glaze. Garnish with extra lemon zest, if desired.
